Career Summary

Shaun Thivierge is a certified security leader with deep expertise in both physical and cyber domains, specializing in protecting critical infrastructure, data centers, and global enterprises. He has led vulnerability management, security technology integration, and incident response programs across industries including energy, finance, and technology. Currently serving as Cyber Security Manager at Oportun, Shaun has driven enterprise-wide remediation efforts achieving PCI DSS compliance, while managing complex vendor ecosystems and deploying advanced SIEM, SOAR, and automation solutions.

With previous roles at PG&E, Bank of the West, and PlayStation, he has overseen large-scale security operations, emergency deployments, and compliance initiatives aligned with ISO 27001, SOC 2, PCI DSS, and NERC-CIP. Shaun holds ASIS board certifications (CPP, PSP, PCI), CompTIA Security+, FAA Part 107, and OSHA 30, and has contributed thought leadership through ASIS and publications in Security Management Magazine. Based in Salt Lake City, he blends strategic vision with hands-on expertise to deliver resilient security programs at scale.

Over a career spanning more than a decade, Shaun has progressed from front-line security operations to senior leadership roles managing global cyber and physical security programs. Beginning as a Flex Officer in 2010, he advanced through GSOC operations, site supervision, and account management before stepping into corporate security advisory roles. His path includes leadership in emergency management during California wildfires, global security systems management, and the convergence of cyber–physical risk programs. Today, as Vulnerability Detection Manager at Oportun, Shaun drives remediation, compliance, and innovation across infrastructure, applications, and vendor ecosystems—while fostering cross-functional collaboration with Legal, HR, Safety, and Facilities.
